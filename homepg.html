What is SmartWardrobe?

SmartWardrobe helps you make informed shopping decisions by checking if you already own similar clothing items. Upload an image of the clothes you're considering purchasing, and we’ll let you know if something like it already exists in your wardrobe.

Upload & Check

Simply upload a photo of the item you're thinking of buying. We’ll compare it to your existing wardrobe and tell you if you already have something similar.

Save Money

Stop buying duplicate items! By using SmartClothes, you can avoid spending money on clothes you already own and make smarter purchasing decisions.

Shop Sustainably

Reduce your environmental footprint by shopping mindfully. SmartClothes encourages thoughtful consumption by helping you recognize what you already have.
